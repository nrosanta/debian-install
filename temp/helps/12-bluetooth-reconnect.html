Helps >
When resuming from suspend or hybernate, a paired bluetooth mouse remains unconnected (unresponsive).
Make a script that runs when going to suspend, like so

cd /etc/pm/sleep.d
sudo nano 10_bluetooth

fill with this 

#!/bin/bash
. /usr/lib/pm-utils/functions

case "$1" in
    hibernate|suspend)
    rfkill block bluetooth
    ;;
    thaw|resume)
    rfkill unblock bluetooth
    ;;
    *)
    ;;
esac
exit

Ctrl-X, y and Enter
then make it executable
sudo chmod 0755 10_bluetooth

On wake up, the bluetooth devices should be reconnected and in about 10 sec will be usable again.
